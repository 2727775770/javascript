Tips:

1. The following Process & Structure are for all interviewers’ reference during the interview to ensure creating a good experience for candidates, further interview evaluation and making decision.

2. The Interview Evaluation Form is for all interviewers’ further usages.

Interview Process & Structure

1.Before Interview
a) double confirm interview criteria with all interviewers
b) read JD(be familiar with Job Responsibilities & requirements)
c) read CV(design questions to unclear/suspicious part and key criteria)
2.Opening
a) Self-introduce
b) Small talk to make a comfort interview environment
c) Introduce interview arrangement
3.Interview Assessment
4.Ending
a) Double check candidate's interest and sale company and position if need
b) Introduce next round interview arrangment plan if nee
c) Leave time for candidate's questions
d) Express thanks and send candidate to leave
5.Interview Evaluation Form
a) Please fill this from asap after interview and sent back to Recruiter within 3 working days
b) Recruiter need upload it intoTalentlink Recruitement
